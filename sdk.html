Offering Developers Innovative Technology

Integrate our card readers with your mobile app by using the Handpoint Software Development Kit

Who says accepting payments has to be complicated?

All of the payment logic is handled by the card reader software so that developers can focus on streamlining the checkout experience for their merchants and customers. Starting a sale transaction is as simple as calling an API function. The best part is that you don't have to be a pro in the payments industry to integrate with our SDKs.

One API to Rule Them All

One single integration to our APIs gives you access to any of Handpoint's card readers.

If a new card reader becomes available, you can start using it right away without having to go through the development process again.

You'll be notified if a new call is available in the SDKs and can decide whether or not to support the new functionality depending on what makes sense for your business.

Simple Documentation

As developers, we prefer to avoid lengthy and convoluted documentation so we can get down to what we really want to do: start coding! With that in mind, we built our documentation around examples, sample apps, and Getting Started guides that are open source. Just copy/paste the code and start trying out the different functionalities.

A Swiss Knife SDK

Our SDK is available on three different platforms: iOS, Android, and Windows. It offers a set of functionalities to developers from starting a sale, refund, or reversal, to offering tips and pre-authorization capabilities to specific industries.

You have control over the transaction result, which gives you the freedom to choose how to handle receipts and other functions.

You design the user experience

No one knows what your merchants need in their point of sale software better than you do. This is why Handpoint strives to make the integration seamless and simple between your app and the card reader so you can focus on enhancing intuitiveness, accessibility, and ease of use between the user and your product.

Security is the Foundation of our technology

All sensitive cardholder data information is encrypted on the card reader and transmitted via secure connections all the way to the acquirer. The fact that your app will never receive any unmasked or unencrypted cardholder data information keeps your software company fully out of the PCI-DSS scope and does not obligate your company to comply with any card security standard.

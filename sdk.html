Offering Developers Innovative Technology

Integrate our card readers with your mobile app by using the Handpoint Software Development Kit

Who says accepting payments has to be complicated?

All of the payment logic is handled by the card reader software so that developers can focus on streamlining the checkout experience for their merchants and customers. Starting a sale transaction is as simple as calling an API function. The best part is that you don't have to be a pro in the payment industry to integrate with our SDKs.

One API to Rule Them All

One single integration to our APIs gives you access to the whole range of Handpoint card readers.

A new card reader is available? You can start using it right away without having to go through the development process again.

A new call is available in the SDKs? You'll be notified of the new release and you can decide to support the new functionality if it makes sense for your business.

Simple Documentation

As developers we tend to want to avoid lengthy and convoluted documentation, and we usually only want one thing: to start coding! With that in mind, we built our documentation around examples, sample apps, and Getting Started guides that are open source. You just have to copy/paste the code and start trying out the different functionalities.

A Swiss Knife SDK

Our SDK is available on three different platforms: iOS, Android, and Windows. It offers a set of functionalities to developers from starting a sale, refund, or reversal, to offering tips and pre-authorization capabilities to specific industries.

You have control over the transaction result, which gives you the freedom to choose how to handle receipts and other functions.

You design the user experience

No one knows what your merchants need in their point of sale software better than you, and this is why Handpoint strives to make the integration seamless and simple between your app and the card reader so that you can focus on enhancing intuitiveness, accessibility, and ease of use between the user and your product.

Security is the Foundation of our technology

All sensitive cardholder data information is encrypted on the card reader and transmitted via secure connections all the way to the acquirer. The fact that your app will never receive any unmasked or unencrypted cardholder data information keeps your software company fully out of the PCI-DSS scope and does not obligate your company to comply with any card security standard.

Handpoint Hi5: Stand-alone + Integrated Mobile

Hi5

Mobile POS for every scenario

Hi5 is the flexible terminal you have been looking for. Standalone or integrated. Countertop or mobile. In the store or in the field. Hi5 does it all.

The cost-effective, P2PE secure Hi5 can be a standalone terminal for merchants whether they are using ANY point of sale software, a cash register, or even no till at all. With GPRS payments, merchants can use the Hi5 anywhere they interact with customers -- at the counter, in the aisle, at the table, or in the field. Like with every Handpoint solution, they’ll have a seamless customer activation and you’ll have robust remote management tools. And, when they upgrade to an integrated POS, you can flip the Hi5 to an integrated terminal with a click of the mouse in the Terminal Management System --with no costs for re-terminalization, no delays for re-keying, no data loss.

For software developers, the Hi5 also has Bluetooth communication and is compatible with all of the Handpoint APIs. The color screen, tokenization, and integrated printer make the Hi5 a powerful mobile or countertop option to offer to merchants on three continents.

Key features:

  • Standalone with GPRS; Integrated with iOS, Android, Windows, Cordova, and Web POS​
  • P2PE payments
  • EMV, NFC, & MSR payments. Tokenization & Tip Adjustment available.​
  • Remote key injection with plug and play activation
  • Integrated printer​
  • Color screen
  • Over-the-air support direct to your card reader, no matter where you take it in the field or in your store.

A Point of Sale Made for You

Handpoint partners with many companies to provide our point of sale systems to a variety of merchants. Our customers have many different needs and whose businesses range from traditional retail and restaurant industries, to bus companies, event ticketing, and pharmacies.

The solutions that we offer make it possible to process payments on multiple platforms such as iOS, Android, and Windows.

Affordable Solutions

Once, it might have been necessary to invest a significant amount of money in a cashier's station, complete with a cumbersome payment terminal, cash register, and computer system. The time has come to leave those outdated methods where they belong: in the past. Handpoint's affordable card readers, bundled with a next generation POS for tablets or smartphones, are bringing payment card processing out of the dark ages and into the 21st century.

Fast & Easy to Use Terminals

Handpoint offers a fully-integrated solution that gives merchants full control over the card reader directly from the point of sale. The setup process is as simple as pairing two devices via bluetooth. This means you can start processing payments within minutes. Our card readers are designed intuitively to provide fast transaction speeds for a fully streamlined experience.

Secure & Reliable Card Readers

In 2006, Handpoint was the first mobile payment company to be certified for point-to-point encryption, and we continue to offer one of the most secure payment platforms on the market. Every transaction is encrypted from the card reader to the banks so you don't have to worry about the security of sensitive data. We take every step to ensure that our solution complies with the latest security standards of the industry.

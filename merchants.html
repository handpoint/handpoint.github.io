A Point of Sale for You

Handpoint partners with many companies that provide point of sale systems to merchants. We have customers that have many different needs and whose businesses range from traditional retail and restaurant industries all the way to bus companies, event ticketing, and pharmacies.

The solutions that we offer will allow you to use our Handpoint card readers to process payments on a wide range of platforms (iOS, Android, and Windows).

Affordable Solutions

At one time, it might have been necessary to spend a lot of money for a cash register, a PC, and a cumbersome payment terminal. The time has come to leave those outdated methods in the past. Handpoint's affordable card readers, bundled with a next generation POS for tablets or smartphones, are bringing payment card processing out of the dark ages and into the 21st century.

Fast & Easy to Use Terminals

Handpoint offers a fully-integrated solution to give merchants full control over the card reader directly from the point of sale. The initial setup is as simple as pairing two devices via bluetooth, which means you can start processing payments within minutes. Our card readers are designed intuitively to provide fast transaction speeds for a fully user-friendly experience.

Secure & Reliable Card Readers

In 2006, Handpoint was the first mobile payment company to be certified for point-to-point encryption, and we continue to offer one of the most secure payment platforms on the market. Every transaction is encrypted from the card reader to the banks so you don't have to worry. We take every step to ensure that our solution complies with the latest security standards of the industry.

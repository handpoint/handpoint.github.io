A Point-of-Sale for You

Handpoint partners with many companies providing point-of-sale systems to merchants. We are working with partners in many market verticals ranging from traditional retail and restaurant industries all the way to bus companies, event ticketing and pharmacies. All solutions that we offer will allow you to use our Handpoint card readers to process payments on a wide range of platforms (iOS, Android and Windows). Do not hesitate to contact our Sales team to get more information about the range of POS we have to offer.

Affordable Solutions

Handpoint's unexpensive card readers bundled with a next generation POS (tablets or smartphones) is bringing card acceptance into the 21st century. Spending a lot of money for a cash register a PC and a bulky payment terminal is over, we provide you with a sleek, compact and reliable solution using state of the art technologies in order to make the cardholder's checkout experience as pleasant as possible.

Fast & Simple

Handpoint offers a fully integrated solution which gives merchants a full control over the card reader directly from the point of sale. The initial setup is as simple as pairing two devices via bluetooth, you can literally start processing payments within minutes. Our card readers are of high quality materials and provide fast transaction speed for an optimized and intuitive user experience.

Secure & Reliable

Handpoint was the first mobile payment company to achieve point to point encryption a few years back and since then we have been building on this base to offer one of the most secure payment platform on the market. Every transaction is encrypted from the card reader to the banks and we make sure to always comply to the latest security standards.

Affordable Solutions

Handpoint's affordable card readers bundled with a next generation POS for tablets or smartphones, are bringing payment card processing into the 21st century. The time when it was standard practice to spend a lot of money for a cash register, a PC, and a bulky payment terminal is long past, and Handpoint will bring you into the future.

We provide you with a sleek, compact, and reliable solution using state-of-the-art technologies in order to make the checkout experience as pleasant as possible for both the merchant and the cardholder.

Fast & Easy to Use Terminals

Handpoint offers a fully-integrated solution which gives merchants a full control over the card reader directly from the point of sale. The initial setup is as simple as pairing two devices via bluetooth, which allows you to start processing payments within minutes. Our card readers are of high quality materials and provide fast transaction speed for an intuitive and user-friendly experience.

Secure & Reliable Card Readers

in 2006, Handpoint was the first mobile payment company to achieve point-to-point encryption, and we have been building on this base to offer one of the most secure payment platform on the market ever since. Every transaction is encrypted from the card reader to the banks and we make sure to always comply to the latest security standards.

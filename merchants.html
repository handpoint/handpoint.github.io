A Point-of-Sale adapted to your needs

The Hilite is Handpoint lightweight and multi purpose card reader. Thought for mobility, the Hilite can be paired with a smartphone application allowing your merchants to accept payments everywhere. We provide Affordable Solutions

The HiPro reader has a sleeve for iPhone/iPod touch 5th generation and iPhone SE as well as an integrated barcode scanner. It offers the possibility for merchants to accept payments around the store without any fixed counter.

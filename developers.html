The Hilite is Handpoint lightweight and multi purpose card reader. Thought for mobility, the Hilite can be paired with a smartphone application allowing your merchants to accept payments everywhere. Complete Set of Features

The HiPro reader has a sleeve for iPhone/iPod touch 5th generation and iPhone SE as well as an integrated barcode scanner. It offers the possibility for merchants to accept payments around the store without any fixed counter. This terminal combines a point of sale, a scanner and a card reader in a unique device to provide the ultimate mobile experience to your customers.

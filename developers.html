Straightforward yet Powerful SDK

As a point of sale provider, you can stop worrying about payments, EMV certifications or long discussions about which processors you want to partner with in order to reach the largest audience of merchants. Handpoint brings a complete solution directly to you, an integration to our API's gives you an instant access to multiple processors around the world as well as pre-certified EMV terminals, ready to accept cardholder's payments.

Complete Set of Features

Our card readers support several types of transactions, basic sales but also refunds and reversals. Handpoint also offers tipping and our team is always working towards adding new functionalities. As an example, authorization only will be an available feature in early 2017. Developers have an extended control over the transaction status and result. For instance, it is simple to add information to a receipt or display the status of a transaction directly on the point of sale for merchants to be able to follow the checkout process. We give you all you need to make your very own payment experience, you just need to focus on what features YOU want to include, and how best to serve your merchants.

Fast Integration

There is power in simplicity and we demonstrate it by offering an API against which you can integrate, literally, in thirty minutes. Half an hour is all you need to be able to start coding and finish the implementation of a complete EMV, NFC or MSR transaction. Carrying a full integration do not take more than a couple of weeks and we make sure to always keep our interface simple and limited to essential functionalities for your business. Speed to market is one of the most important factor for success and we make sure to always keep this in mind when designing our products.

POS in a BOX

Once you have carried out the integration, we want to make sure that the distribution and activation of your point-of-sale with the Handpoint card readers is smooth and that our bundled offer is on top of the merchants expectations. To achieve this result we work with you to find the best approach to bring the end product to market. Whether we send the card readers separately or offer the full POS solution in a bundled package, we always adapt to your needs.

Start Playing Around

When starting integrating against our API's you have two solutions, you can either order a development card reader and test cards by contacting Handpoint directly or you can start by using our card reader simulator available online for FREE. Sample apps and getting started guides are available on our developers website and we recommend that you download our simple mPOS app from the Google Play Store or Apple Store in order to get familiar with our solution and the functionalities we provide.

Excellent Integration Support

At Handpoint, we make sure to follow your integration project closely and we always have a resource available to help if ever you encounter problems with using our SDKs, we're here to help and focus on the success of our partners.

A Simple and Powerful SDK

As a point of sale provider, you can stop worrying about EMV certifications and the lengthy process of choosing which processors to partner with to reach the largest audience of merchants. Handpoint simplifies the process and brings a complete solution directly to you. Through our Software Development Kit (SDK) We offer instant access to pre-certified EMV terminals as well as multiple processors around the world.

Complete Set of Features

Our payment terminals support several types of functionalities and payment types:

  • Sale (MSR, EMV, NFC, PIN Debit)
  • Refund
  • Reversal
  • Tipping
  • Pre-Authorizations (2017)

Developers have extensive control over the transaction result and adding information to a receipt or displaying the status of a transaction directly on the point of sale is simple. We give you everything you need to create a payment experience that's personalized for your merchants.

Fast Integration

There's power in simplicity. Handpoint offers an API that can be integrated against in only 30 minutes, giving you everything you need to implement a simple EMV, NFC, or MSR sale transaction from start to finish.

Carrying a full integration doesn't take more than a couple of weeks, and we make sure that our interface remains simple and limited to only the most essential functionalities necessary for your business.

Reduced Time to Market

Once you've carried out the integration, we make sure to facilitate the distribution and activation of your point of sale with the Handpoint card readers.

We'll work with you to find the best approach to bring your product to market. Whether we send the card readers separately or offer the full POS solution in a bundled package, we can adapt to suit your needs.

Start Coding Now !

To begin integrating, follow these two simple steps:

  1. Download our SDKs and import the libraries into your project. At this point, you can start playing around with our card reader simulator.
  2. Contact Handpoint directly to order your first payment terminal and test cards in order to carry out a full integration.

Our sample apps and getting started guides are also available on our developers website. We recommend that you download our own mPOS app from the Google Play Store or Apple Store to get familiar with our solution and the functionalities we provide.

Excellent Integration Support

At Handpoint, we're focused on the success of our partners and will follow the progress of your integration project closely every step of the way. If you run into any problems when using our SDKs, we're more than happy to help.

Handpoint provides other support resources, and our support team is prepared to asssist with troubleshooting and advice if needed.

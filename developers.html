Simple Yet Powerful SDK

As a point of sale provider, you can stop worrying about payments, EMV certifications, and long discussions about which processors you want to partner with in order to reach the largest audience of merchants.

Handpoint brings a complete solution directly to you. An integration to our APIs gives you an instant access to multiple processors around the world as well as pre-certified EMV terminals, so you can be ready to accept payments as quickly as possible.

Complete Set of Features

Our payment terminals support several types of functionalities and payment types :

  • Sale (MSR, EMV, NFC, PIN Debit)
  • Refund
  • Reversal
  • Tipping
  • Authorization only (2017)

Developers have extensive control over the transaction status and result. It's easy to add information to a receipt or display the status of a transaction directly on the point of sale so merchants can follow the checkout process. We provide everything you need to make a payment experience that's personalized for your merchants.

Fast Integration

There is power in simplicity and we demonstrate this concept by offering an API that can be integrated against in only 30 minutes. It is all you need from start to finish in order to implement a simple EMV, NFC, or MSR sale transaction.

Carrying a full integration doesn't take more than a couple of weeks, and we make sure that our interface remains simple and limited to only the most essential functionalities necessary for your business.

Reduced Time to Market

Once you have carried out the integration, we make sure to facilitate the distribution and activation of your point of sale with the Handpoint card readers.

To ensure that everything goes as smoothly as possible, we'll work with you to find the best approach to bring the end product to market. Whether we send the card readers separately or offer the full POS solution in a bundled package, we can adapt to suit your needs.

Start the Integration

In order to start the integration you have two solutions :

  • Order a development card reader and test cards by contacting Handpoint directly
  • Use our card reader simulator which is available for free when downloading our SDKs.

Sample apps and getting started guides are also available on our developers website and we recommend that you download our simple mPOS app from the Google Play Store or Apple Store in order to get familiar with our solution and the functionalities we provide.

Excellent Integration Support

Handpoint provides other support resources, and our support team is prepared to asssist with troubleshooting and advice if needed. We strive to make developing point of sale software as painless as possible.

What do we do ?

At Handpoint, we are tech-enablers, and our role is to offer a simple interface so developers can add integrated card payment solutions into their mobile, tablet, or PC point-of-sale.

We developed beautifully simple APIs for our sleek and modern card readers while taking an extra step in security by being the first to implement a PCI-P2PE solution for mobile point of sale.

By building simple yet powerful products, we allow developers to focus on delivering a seamless experience to their users, without having to spend months on integrations and payment certifications.

Company Facts

Founded

Handpoint was founded in Iceland in 1999 to develop software on the first generation of PALM devices.

the company has greatly evolved since then but has always believed in the power of mobile technologies.

That is why, since 2009, the company has been focusing on mobile payments.

Core Business

We power payments for the next Generation of point of sale systems (mobile & tablets) #NextGenPos.

We have mastered EMV, contactless and point to point encryption to offer a secure and simple solution for our partners.

Our goal is to make accepting card payments a piece of cake.

Products

Sleek card readers which allow merchants to start accepting card payments within minutes.

An API for developers to integrate their mobile applications with Handpoint card readers.

A payment gateway with connections to multiple processors around the world.

Funding raised

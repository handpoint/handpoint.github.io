Congratulations for joining
the Handpoint mobile POS family!

Here are five steps to help you get started with your new mobile payment solution

1. Downloading the Handpoint App

The first step that needs to be taken is to download the Handpoint App onto your smartphone. Just search for "Handpoint" in the store and the app should appear at the top of the list.

  • Play Store for Android
  • App Store for iOS

If you do not have access to the Play Store / App Store, you will have to start by creating an account to be able to download the app.

2. First Time Login

The first time the Handpoint App is opened, a log-in passcode must be created. This passcode will be used to log-in to the Handpoint App. The passcode can be changed at any time under "settings" in the Handpoint App.

3. Activating your App with the Shared Secret

The shared secret is a unique key shared between your app and the card reader which prevents potential hackers from connecting to your payment terminal. This unique key must have been sent to your personal email address in a text file.

In order to activate your app with the shared secret you need to open the text file through the email client on your smartphone or tablet.

Note : If you are using an Android device it is necessary to completely close and log out of the Handpoint app before activating the shared secret.

Activating the shared secret is easy:

  1. Open the e-mail from Handpoint using the email client in your smartphone / tablet, click and hold the file attached for few seconds and choose to open the attachment in the Handpoint app.
  2. The app will prompt: "Do you want to activate your shared secret?“ (android) or "shared secret saved“ (iOS), Choose "OK" if needed.
  3. Log into the Handpoint app with your four digit passcode to finalise the activation.

4. Connecting your Card Reader and the App

The next step is to connect your card reader to your smartphone / tablet. You only need to do this once, after which the two devices will connect automatically when the Handpoint app is running (as long as the card reader is turned on).

iPhone / iPad

  1. Make sure bluetooth is turned on.
  2. Go to the menu screen of the app and select Connect.
  3. In the upper right corner select Discover. Your device will now search for the card reader.
  4. Select your card reader from the list.
  5. Select Pin Pad.

Android devices

  1. Make sure bluetooth is turned on.
  2. Go to the menu screen of the app and select Connect to Card Reader.
  3. Select Bluetooth Settings.
  4. Select your card reader from the list.
  5. Your device will ask you if you are sure you want to pair the devices, Select Pair.
<

5. Card Reader Initialization

To initialise the card reader you need to start your first transaction. right after initiating a sale the reader will update its software and configuration.

  1. Enter an amount onto the payment screen on the Handpoint app and press Pay.
  2. The card reader will now start the update itself. This process can take one to two minutes depending on the internet connection.

The connection between the app and the reader will be lost during this update process. The connection will be re-established when the next transaction is made.

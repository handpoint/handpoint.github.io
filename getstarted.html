Congratulations for joining
the Handpoint mobile POS family!

Here are five steps to help you get started with your new mobile payment solution

Downloading the Handpoint App

Handpoint partners with many companies that provide point of sale systems to merchants. We work with partners that have many different needs and whose businesses range from traditional retail and restaurant industries all the way to bus companies, event ticketing, and pharmacies. The solutions that we offer will allow you to use our Handpoint card readers to process payments on a wide range of platforms (iOS, Android, and Windows). Feel free to contact our Sales team for more information on the range of POS we have to offer.

First Time Login

Handpoint's affordable card readers bundled with a next generation POS for tablets or smartphones, are bringing payment card processing into the 21st century. The time when it was standard practice to spend a lot of money for a cash register, a PC, and a bulky payment terminal is long past, and Handpoint will bring you into the future. We provide you with a sleek, compact, and reliable solution using state-of-the-art technologies in order to make the checkout experience as pleasant as possible for both the merchant and the cardholder.

Activating your App with the Shared Secret

Handpoint offers a fully-integrated solution which gives merchants a full control over the card reader directly from the point of sale. The initial setup is as simple as pairing two devices via bluetooth, which allows you to start processing payments within minutes. Our card readers are of high quality materials and provide fast transaction speed for an intuitive and user-friendly experience.

Connecting your Card Reader and the App

Every transaction is encrypted from the card reader to the banks and we make sure to always comply to the latest security standards.

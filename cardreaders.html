The HiLite

The HiLite is Handpoint's multi-purpose lightweight card reader. Designed with mobility in mind, the HiLite can be paired with a smartphone payment application, allowing your merchants to accept payments virtually anywhere. Its simple, elegant design ensures that it's equally at home whether it's in hand or in its stand, on-the-go or at a checkout station.

HiLite Specifications

The HiLite is the most compact and lightweight Bluetooth enabled versatile mobile payment solution. It accepts all forms of payment: Magnetic stripe, EMV Chip & PIN and Contactless cards and this way delivers power and usability in the smallest convenient and stylish device that fits in the palm of your hand.The HiLite meets the latest EMV and PCI PTS 3.x specifications for encrypting PINentry devices.

The HiPro card reader accomodates 5th generation iPhone/iPod touch as well as the iPhone SE. Complete with an integrated barcode scanner, it's an all-in-one payment solution that allows merchants to get out from behind the checkout counter for more personal customer interactions.

Accept all payment types

The HiLite and the HiPro can process payments via: Magnetic Stripe, Chip & Sign, Chip & Pin, Contactless and support Apple and Android Pay. Handpoint always stays on top of the latest technologies in order to offer the best checkout experience to both the merchant and the cardholder.

The Single Charger

Mostly used for mobile scenarios, the low-profile Single Charger allows the card reader to be removed from the cradle so it can be brought directly to the customer.

The Stand

Designed for fixed point of sale solutions, the stand fits perfectly on a countertop, a discreet charging cable on the side of the stand will keep the terminal charged at all times.

The Multi Charger

Perfect for larger retailers and restaurants, the Multi Charger allows multiple readers to be charged at the same time and is often used for queue busting scenarios.

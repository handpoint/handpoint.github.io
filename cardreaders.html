The HiLite

The HiLite is Handpoint's multi-purpose lightweight card reader. Designed with mobility in mind, the HiLite can be paired with a smartphone payment application, allowing your merchants to accept payments virtually anywhere. This elegantly-designed card reader fits in perfectly in its stand right next to a tablet or a computer, but it's just as at home in hand with its ergonomic design. Above all, the HiLite is remarkably easy to use and well-suited for a wide variety of needs.

The HiPro

The HiPro card reader fits 5th generation iPhone/iPod touch as well as the iPhone SE. Complete with an integrated barcode scanner, it's an all-in-one payment solution that allows merchants to get out from behind the checkout counter for more personal customer interactions. This terminal has it all: barcode scanner, EMV and MagStripe card reader capabilities, and with a compatible mobile device and mPOS app, you will never want to go back to old-fashioned fixed terminal payment processing again.

Future Device : The HiFive

Handpoint’s offerings are constantly evolving to suit the needs of our partners. That is why we will be excited to welcome the newest member to our card reader family in 2017: the HiFive. It will combine the portability and multi-functionality of the HiLite with the capability of WiFi connectivity. Not only that, but the HiFive comes complete with an integrated printer so that you’ll never be tied down to a separate cashier station receipt printer ever again!

Card Readers Accessories

The Stand

Designed for fixed point of sale solutions, the stand fits perfectly on a countertop, a discreet charging cable is available on the side of the stand in order to keep the terminal charged at all times.

The Single Charger

Mostly used for mobile scenarios, the low-profile Single Charger allows the card reader to be removed from the cradle so it can be brought directly to the customer.

The Multi Charger

Perfect for larger retailers and restaurants, the Multi Charger allows multiple readers to be charged at the same time and is often used for queue busting scenarios.

Accept all payment types

The HiLite and the HiPro can process payments via: Magnetic Stripe, Chip & Sign, Chip & Pin, Contactless and support Apple and Android Pay. Handpoint always stays on top of the latest technologies in order to offer the best checkout experience to both the merchant and the cardholder.

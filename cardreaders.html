Aesthetic, compact and designed for your needs

The Hilite

The Hilite is Handpoint lightweight and multi purpose card reader. Thought for mobility, the Hilite can be paired with a smartphone application allowing your merchants to accept payments everywhere. Designed with elegance it will also fit perfectly on a stand right next to a tablet or a computer. The terminal is ergonomic and above all is remarkably easy to use, it will most likely accomodate a large range of your merchants.

The Hipro

The HiPro reader has a sleeve for iPhone/iPod touch 5th generation and iPhone SE as well as an integrated barcode scanner. It offers the possibility for merchants to accept payments around the store without any fixed counter. This terminal combines a point of sale, a scanner and a card reader in a unique device to provide the ultimate mobile experience to your customers.

Accept all payment types

The HiLite and the HiPro read Magnetic Stripe, Chip & Sign, Chip & Pin, Contactless and support Apple and Android Pay. Handpoint always stay on top of the latest technologies in order to offer the best checkout experience to both the merchant and the cardholder.

Accessories

Handpoint offers three different types of accessories for the card readers :

The stand designed for fixed point of sale solutions, fits perfectly on a counter top, a discreet charging cable is available on the side of the stand in order to keep the terminal charged at all times.

The single charger mostly used for mobile scenarios, allows the card reader to be removed from the cradle and brought directly to the customer.

The multi-charger used for larger retailers or restaurants, allows multiple readers to be charged at the same time and is often used for queue busting scenarios.

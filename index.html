Enabling Hardware

Say  to our card readers

Accepting credit and debit card payments has never been so easy. Our unique EMV pre-certified terminals adapt to your needs whether you'd prefer a fixed point of sale station or a mobile device. Wherever there's access to the internet (wifi, 4G, 3G etc...) you can accept card payments. Magstripe, contactless, and chip cards are supported as well as Apple Pay and Android Pay.

AND

Enabling Software

Control the checkout experience

Our Software Development Kit (SDK), available on three platforms, iOS, Android and Windows, grants POS developers full control over the card reader so they can deliver an integrated experience to their merchants. Handpoint's SDKs offer a quick integration and an easy route to market for companies wanting to add card payment capabilites to their mobile or fixed point of sale (POS) solutions.

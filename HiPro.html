Handpoint HiPro: Complete Mobile Point of Sale

HiPro NFC

Complete Mobile Point of Sale

Handpoint HiPro NFC puts a full Point of Sale in the palm of your hand. Engage with your customers everywhere: scan items and take payments in the aisle, place orders tableside, scan ticket barcodes at events and parking garages, and more. One solution for barcode scanning, your point of sale software, and P2PE + EMV/NFC/MSR payments and tokenization, with remote key injection, easy APIs, and certifications on three continents. Go mobile | Go global.

Key features:

  • iPhone / iPod touch sled​​
  • 1D/2D barcode scanner​
  • P2PE semi-integrated payments
  • EMV, NFC, & MSR payments. Tokenization & Tip Adjustment available.​
  • Remote key injection with plug and play activation​
  • Lightning connection​
  • Charge both phone and sled at once with USB cord or charging docks​
  • >2000 transactions per charge​
  • Over-the-air support direct to your card reader, no matter where you take it in the field or in your store.​

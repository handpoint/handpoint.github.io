International Partnership

The Handpoint Gateway has direct connections to processors in Europe, United States, and South Africa. We offer our partners the chance to easily deploy their point of sale solution on an international scale by allowing them to use multiple processors in any region currently supported.

Secure and reliable Gateway

Security and fraud prevention are our top priorities. The Handpoint Gateway is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified, which means that cardholders sensitive data remains protected according to the latest security standards. Our Gateway is hosted on Amazon Web Services. This makes it easy to scale our solution around the world while ensuring an uptime close to 100%.

Handpoint HiPlus is our latest P2PE mobile payment terminal. This sleek standalone with 3G and a color touch screen lets you deliver an incredible customer experience. The HiPlus makes mobile sales appealing AND cost-effective with all the security and seamless merchant support enabled by the Handpoint platform.

Key features:​

  • Color touch screen ​
  • Use it as stand-alone with 3G, no app needed
  • P2PE security ​
  • EMV, NFC, & MSR payments
  • Remote key injection with plug and play activation ​
  • Receipts sent via SMS text message
  • WiFi or 3G connectivity​
  • >500 transactions per charge​
  • Over-the-air support direct to your card reader, no matter where you take it in the field or in your store​​

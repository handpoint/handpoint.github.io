International Partnership

The Handpoint Gateway has direct connections to processors in Europe, United States and South Africa. We offer to our partners the possibility to deploy easily their point of sale solution on an international scale by allowing them to use multiple processors in any region currently supported. We strive everyday to expand our network in order to offer more choices to our partners.

Secure and reliable Gateway

THe Handpoint Gateway is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified which means that cardholder sensitive data is always protected according to the latest security standards in order to prevent fraud and potential hacks. Our Gateway is hosted on Amazon Web Services, making it easy to scale our solution around the world and ensuring an uptime close to 100%.

Processors Functionalities
